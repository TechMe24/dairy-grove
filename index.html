Dole Nutrition Info

Dole Brand Soft Serve only markets their fruit flavors, but the same great company that has always made Dole Chocolate & Vanilla flavors continues to make them and that's how we bring them to you.

The Dole Fruit Flavors are Lactose-Free Fat-Free, Gluten-Free, Dairy-Free and Vegan.

The Chocolate & Vanilla Flavors are Gluten-Free and Lactose-Free but NOT Dairy Free.

Coconut, Banana, and Buttermint Flavors not listed; Product made with Vanilla Dole as a base.
